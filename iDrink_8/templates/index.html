Welcome to iDrink
Let's get your bartender set up in two quick steps — assign your bottles, then create your first drink.
What you'll need
• The bottles connected to each pump
• A drink recipe in parts (e.g. 2 parts vodka, 1 part juice)
Assign your bottles
Enter what's connected to each pump. Leave blank for unused pumps.
Tip: use simple names like "Vodka", "OJ", "Tonic" — these drive the colour and recipe suggestions.
Create your first drink
Give it a name and set how many parts to pour from each pump. Leave pumps at 0 to skip them.
Parts are relative — 2 parts + 1 part pours twice as much of the first ingredient.
🎉
You're all set!
Your bottles are assigned and your first drink is ready to pour. You can add more drinks and menus any time in the Admin tab.
